Our Mission.

Since expressing creativity for both students and adults alike is important for bonding together, education, and for expressing experiences, stories, and emotions, we decided to create from scratch a desmos art generator. We plan to make our web app accessible, convenient, and affordable to everyone.

100% Free

Because our mission is to make STEM+art accessible to all, our services and web app are 100% free to every user.

Creative Expressions

Art is shown to be a healthy way to let out emotions; by utilizing our art generator, everyone, even those who don't feel like they're artistically inclined, can produce a piece of art that represents either themselves, their experiences, stories, and/or emotions.

Bonding Environment

Just like how a sport, hobby, or activity can bond individuals together, so can art. By providing equal access to our generator, we hope to ensure that everyone has the chance to talk about and create artwork together.
